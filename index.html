Welcome to Lister Community School’s EAL Assessment. EAL stands for English as an Additional Language.

This assessment will help your teacher to find your current level and make it easier to give you extra help when you need it.* There are four sections - Reading, Writing, Speaking and Listening. You’ll know your results immediately so there’s nothing to worry about.

Raise your hand and ask your teacher for help if you don’t understand any part of the assessment or have any questions.

*Your result can be checked against the 2016 DfE Codes (A-E).
